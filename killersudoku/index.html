Rules:

The goal is to fill the grid, so that each row, each column and each region of 3x3 boxes contains all the numbers from 1 to 9.

The number at the top of the first box of each dotted region corresponds to the sum of the digits of the boxes of that region.

For example a region of two cases with the number 5 can be broken down into 1+4 or 2+3.
